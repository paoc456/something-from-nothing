Poem

The newly ripe
cherries scatter like
coins of elm seeds.

It is also April in
Yangzhou.

Last night red
orchids in the
thatched hut burst
into blossom

Worrying about the
wind and rain
[that might ruin the blossoms],
unable to sleep.

Birds and Flowers
Huang Shen

Artwork

The spray of red
orchid sketched with
swift brushstrokes in
red and green
is cropped
at the top.
A calligraphic inscription is
integrated with the painting into
an abstract design,
leaving empty space
in the left half of
the painting.
